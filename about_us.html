Our Mission

Sky trackers Association was founded in 2016 with one mission : spread passion for astronomy.

Since then our team of dedicated volunteers have organized over 200 events gathering adults, teenagers and even younger curious minds sharing fascination for the night sky.

Whether your are discovering or already an astronomer enthusiast you are welcome at our meetings to ask your questions or share simply your discoveries. All are welcome !
Thanks to our volunteers and partners we have material for you to discover the deep sky. We help young astronomers choose and set their own material should they want to go a step further in tracking celestial objects.

We do organize and host our own meetings but also private or scholar groups. Should you want to plan a simple visit or wish to organize specific arrangements, just fill in the contact form below.
